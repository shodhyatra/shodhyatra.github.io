This is our humble attempt at creating a Shodh Yatra website that can be used to benefit many groups in the future as well.

Made by Harshit, Somesh, Somshekhar and Suroor

Features of the Website:

  • Includes a page for blog of each Shodh Yatra.
  • A page for each of the amazing students found during the Yatra. The page can be used by the student to get mentorship from various people.
  • A page where all the academic content created by the Shodh Yatris (in the past as well as future) can be shared.
